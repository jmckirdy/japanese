Little Tsu

  • A double consonant is represented with the small っ. A small っ is silent but makes the prior consonent be timed for two moras instead of one. eg:
    みっか  (mikka)     ==>     [mi] + [silent] + [ka]
    みか (mika)     ==>     [mi] + [ka]
    みつか (mitsuka)     ==>     [mi] + [tsu] + [ka]
